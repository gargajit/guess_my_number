About the Game

  • The game starts with a score of 20.
  • Each wrong guess decreases your score by 1.
  • Your initial high score is 0.
  • When you guess the number correctly, your score becomes your high score if it is higher than your previous high score.
  • If your guess is too low, you'll see a "Too Low" message. Similarly, if your guess is too high, you'll see a "Too High" message.
  • Clicking the Again button resets the game, including the score and the secret number, but your high score remains to beat.
